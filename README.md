## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**What is Magpie?**
Magpie is a productivity app that rewards you with coins for completing habits and tasks. Use your coins to claim rewards you set for yourself.

**How do I earn coins?**
Complete daily habits and finish tasks to earn coins. Each habit and task has a coin value you can customise.

**How do I spend coins?**
Create rewards in the Rewards tab, set a coin cost, and claim them when you've earned enough.

### Habits

**How do I create a habit?**
Tap the + button on the Habits tab and enter your habit details.

**How do I mark a habit complete?**
Tap on the day in the habit calendar to mark it complete and earn coins.

**Can I track habits for previous days?**
Yes, tap any day in the calendar to mark it complete or incomplete.

### Tasks

**How do I add subtasks?**
When creating or editing a task, add subtasks to break it into smaller steps.

**What happens when I complete all subtasks?**
The task is marked complete and you earn the coins.

### Notes

**How do I organise notes?**
Use categories to organise your notes. You can create custom categories in the app.

**Can I search my notes?**
Yes, use the search bar at the top of the Notes tab.

### Syncing & Data

**How do I sync across devices?**
Sign in to iCloud on all devices with the same Apple ID. Your data syncs automatically.

**Why isn't my data syncing?**
Make sure iCloud is enabled in your device settings and you have an internet connection.

**How do I delete my data?**
Go to Settings > Reset All Data in the app.

### Subscriptions

**What does Pro include?**
Pro unlocks unlimited habits, tasks, rewards, categories, custom colours, and statistics.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.

**How do I restore my purchase?**
Go to the Upgrade screen and tap "Restore Purchases".

### Troubleshooting

**The app is running slowly**
Try closing and reopening the app, or restart your device.

**My coins aren't showing correctly**
Your coins are calculated from your completed habits and tasks. If something looks wrong, try refreshing the app.
